# Example printability reports

Generated by `./generate-examples.sh` with `--cell 4` (A1 Mini: 180×180×180 mm).

Two ways to pass the gates:

- **Gusset mode** (default): every Life birth leans on its three B3 parents
  via small diagonal braces — the whole stack is self-supporting, one piece,
  nothing to remove. Any interesting pattern qualifies, including gliders.
- **Breakaway trees** (relaxed): rest-on-model landings are double-tapered
  needles now, and up to 2 are allowed — long-lived soups pass again.

All examples must be `interesting OK` (no still life / short oscillator
before generation ≥ max(8, ⅓·depth)).
