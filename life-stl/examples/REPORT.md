# Example printability reports

Generated by `./generate-examples.sh` with `--cell 4`.

Default supports are **breakaway**. Tree style merges nearby tips onto
shared trunks (Cura/Bambu-style); pillar style keeps one shaft per tip.
After snapping them off, Life must be face-connected to the bed (no orphans).
