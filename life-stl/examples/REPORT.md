# Example printability reports

Generated by `./generate-examples.sh` with `--cell 4` (A1 Mini: 180×180×180 mm).

All examples use **gusset mode** (default): every Life birth leans on its
three B3 parents via small diagonal braces, so the whole stack is
self-supporting, prints as one piece, and needs no support cleanup.

Every example must stay **active for its entire printed height** — patterns
that settle into a still life or short oscillator partway up are rejected
(they extrude a boring static tower above the interesting part).
